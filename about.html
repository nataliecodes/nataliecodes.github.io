About Me

I'm Natalie and this is my (first) website. Welcome!

I made this site to show off my newfound skills as a software developer. I'm currently enrolled in Dev Bootcamp's 18-week web development immersive.

Before DBC, I went to Northwestern University where I majored in Communication Studies and International Studies. I worked in social media production at a magazine and then was at an environmental law firm for over 2 years. Neither of these jobs were right for me, so I began to research other career paths.

I found codecademy and the rest is history. I fell in love with coding. I love details, problem-solving, and puzzles. Once I graduate from DBC, I hope to join a company as a full-stack developer.

Outside of coding... I follow more dogs than humans on instagram and I love farmers markets and walking around new places.
