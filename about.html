about me

i'm natalie and this is my (first) website. welcome!

i made this site to show off my newfound skills as a software developer. i am currently enrolled in dev bootcamp's 18-week web development bootcamp.

before dev, i went to northwestern university where i majored in communication studies and international studies. i worked in social media production at a magazine and then was at an environmental law firm for over 2 years. i quickly realized i didn't want a career in social media or law. after researching several other career paths, i found codecademy and fell in love with coding. i love details, problem-solving, and puzzles. once i graduate from dev, i hope to join a company as a full-stack developer.

outside of coding... i follow more dogs than humans on instagram, i love farmers markets, and walking around new places.
